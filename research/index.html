Home/Search and browse

Research

Browse JORN Open Research Records.

This catalogue will list formally accepted JORN publications and expose filters for research objects, review routes, implementation dimensions, badges and current status.

No JORN records have been published. JORN is in a founding and feasibility phase. Repository deposits and pilot proposals will not appear as journal publications.

Planned catalogue fields¶

Readers should be able to filter by title and abstract, author and ORCID, affiliation and ROR, discipline, article type, date, review route, object type, access category, implementation level by dimension, evidence badge, external assessment, criteria version, and current status.

Search results must distinguish accepted publications from corrections, expressions of concern, retractions, illustrative examples and non-public pilot materials. Filters will not rank scientific worth, institutional prestige, predicted impact or openness with a composite score.

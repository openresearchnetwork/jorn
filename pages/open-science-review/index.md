## Review scope

Reviewers examine identifiers, versions, repositories, metadata, licences, access terms, documentation, citation, provenance, relationships, accessibility, preservation, restrictions and implementation levels. They check evidence for the exact package rather than infer practices from author reputation or institutional resources.

## Outputs

The report records assessor, date, criteria version, exact objects, evidence inspected, level by applicable dimension, differences, exceptions, exclusions, human judgments and limitations. Authors can respond and deposit corrected versions. Only confirmed findings move to the published profile.

## Boundaries

Open-science review does not establish methodological validity, correctness, novelty, ethical approval, software function, reproduced results, privacy safety or independent replication unless a separately qualified assessment explicitly covers that claim.

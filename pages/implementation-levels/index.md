<div class="info-banner"><strong>Status: draft criteria.</strong> Levels are proposed for the founding pilot. They are not grades, prestige measures, or claims about scientific correctness.</div>

## The four levels

| Level | Name | Minimum meaning | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **0** | Not documented | The object or practice is missing, unclear, or cannot be assessed. | Gap recorded; reason where known |
| **1** | Transparent | Existence, location, access status, owner, restrictions, limitations, and non-availability are disclosed. | Versioned statement and links |
| **2** | Accessible, citable, and responsibly managed | The object is appropriately deposited or registered, persistently identified, versioned, documented, connected, and governed by a licence or clear access terms. | Exact object, metadata, identifier, version, access and preservation evidence |
| **3** | Independently verified | JORN or a recognised external evaluator examined the exact object using published criteria. | Assessor, date, criteria, report, results, differences, exclusions and limits |

Controlled or restricted materials may reach Level 2 when the restriction is justified and a responsible access or verification route exists. Level 3 is not automatically “better science”; it records an independent assessment of the specified implementation evidence.

## Dimensional profile

The profile assesses independently: manuscript; identities and contributor roles; affiliations; protocol and preregistration; data and synthetic data; software and models; environment, workflows and notebooks; figures and media; methods and materials; licences; identifiers, versioning, citation and provenance; ethics, consent and governance; accessibility; scientific review; open-science review; artifact evaluation; reproduction; replication; maintenance and preservation.

| Dimension | Applicable? | Level | Verification state | Exact evidence | Limitations |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Data | Yes / no | 0–3 | Self / supplied / AI-screened / JORN verified | Identifier and version | Access or scope limits |
| Software | Yes / no | 0–3 | As above | Release, source and environment | Platform or test limits |
| Scientific review | Yes | 0–3 | JORN verified before publication | Reports, response and decision | Expertise and disclosure limits |

If JORN later displays an overall implementation designation, it will be the minimum level across applicable mandatory dimensions—not an average.

> This designation describes implementation of the JORN Open Science Framework. It is not a measure of scientific quality, importance, correctness, novelty, or impact.

## Measures JORN will not issue

JORN will not publish numerical openness scores, percentages of trust, stars, A–F grades, rankings, prestige or predicted-impact measures, or one combined measure of scientific quality and openness.

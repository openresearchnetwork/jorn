## Access, rights, and fees

JORN is intended to be Diamond Open Access: no subscriptions, paywalls, article-processing charges, or mandatory publication fees. Authors retain copyright. CC BY 4.0 is the preferred default license for article content; data and software should use suitable open licenses or dedications.

## Peer review and editorial decisions

Research articles will normally receive at least two independent reviews. Reviewer expertise, methodological fit, conflicts, institutional independence, and diversity of perspective will inform selection. Final decisions belong to an unconflicted editor and will not be controlled by sponsors or partners.

## Research integrity

The final framework will address plagiarism, fabrication and falsification, image or data manipulation, authorship disputes, undisclosed conflicts, corrections, expressions of concern, retractions, and withdrawal. JORN will remain responsible for the integrity of its certification even when files are hosted elsewhere.

## Evidence, data, and software

Authors should make supporting research objects available in appropriate repositories with persistent identifiers, licenses, documentation, and exact versions. Legitimate restrictions involving privacy, consent, security, or community governance must be explained and handled through an approved access route—not ignored.

## Accessibility {#accessibility}

The journal aims to meet WCAG 2.2 AA as its public baseline. Pages should support keyboard navigation, visible focus, semantic structure, sufficient contrast, responsive layouts, meaningful alternative text, and reduced-motion preferences. Accessibility issues will have a clear reporting route before launch.

## Versioning and accountability

Final policies will display an effective date, revision history, and responsible governance body. Material changes will be announced and preserved so that authors and reviewers can determine which rules applied to a particular editorial record.

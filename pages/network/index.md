## The relationship

The **Open Research Network** is the broader community and collaboration initiative. **JORN** is its proposed peer-review and scholarly-certification program. **Open Science Labs** supports the development of both.

This structure is intended to welcome contributors who often sit across institutional boundaries: independent researchers, open-source maintainers, research software engineers, data scientists, librarians, reproducibility initiatives, and disciplinary communities.

## Partner principles

Partnership does not confer editorial privilege. Any formal relationship should be transparent, mission-aligned, and governed by written safeguards for editorial independence. JORN should prefer partners that support open standards, portable data, persistent identifiers, and community accountability.

Potential connections may include open repositories, scholarly identifier services, preservation networks, universities, libraries, research-software communities, and independent research organizations. Names will only be presented as partners after a relationship is confirmed.

      <p class="eyebrow">One scholarly record</p>
      <h2>Everything needed to understand, verify, and build on the work.</h2>
      <p>Every JORN publication will be a persistent certification record: the authoritative map between an accepted manuscript, the evidence behind it, and the people and process that evaluated it.</p>
      <ul class="check-list">
        <li>Exact reviewed and accepted versions</li>
        <li>Linked data, software, protocols, and preregistrations</li>
        <li>Editorial decisions, reviews, and author responses</li>
        <li>Machine-readable metadata and persistent identifiers</li>
      </ul>

<section class="section workflow-section">
  <div class="shell section-heading section-heading--split">
    <div>
      <p class="eyebrow">How publishing works</p>
      <h2>Rigorous review.<br>A lighter footprint.</h2>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p>Authors begin by sharing their work in an appropriate open repository. JORN focuses on the work a journal uniquely must do: editorial assessment, peer review, certification, and stewardship.</p>
      <a class="text-link" href="editorial-process/">Full editorial process <span aria-hidden="true">→</span></a>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="shell workflow">
    <article><span>1</span><h3>Deposit</h3><p>Share the manuscript and research objects in trusted open repositories.</p></article>
    <article><span>2</span><h3>Connect</h3><p>Provide persistent links for data, software, protocols, identities, and funding.</p></article>
    <article><span>3</span><h3>Review</h3><p>Editors and independent peers assess validity, transparency, and reproducibility.</p></article>
    <article><span>4</span><h3>Certify</h3><p>JORN identifies the accepted version and publishes the complete editorial record.</p></article>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="section principles-section">
  <div class="shell section-heading section-heading--center">
    <p class="eyebrow">Our publishing principles</p>
    <h2>Open for readers. Fair for authors.<br>Accountable to the community.</h2>
  </div>
  <div class="shell principle-grid">
    <article><strong>No APCs</strong><h3>Diamond Open Access</h3><p>No subscriptions, paywalls, or mandatory publication fees.</p></article>
    <article><strong>Author-owned</strong><h3>Rights retained</h3><p>Authors keep copyright; CC BY 4.0 is the preferred article license.</p></article>
    <article><strong>Community-led</strong><h3>Transparent governance</h3><p>Roles, decisions, funding, partnerships, and policy changes are public.</p></article>
    <article><strong>Interoperable</strong><h3>Open infrastructure</h3><p>Open-source tools, public metadata, persistent identifiers, and exportable records.</p></article>
  </div>
</section>

## Before proposing work

Use the [Implementation Guide](../guide/) and [Readiness Check](../readiness/). Deposit and version the manuscript and applicable research objects; prepare identifiers and licences or access terms; document restrictions, missing elements and next actions; and identify discipline-specific expertise.

## Package checklist

Provide authors, ORCIDs, affiliations, RORs and CRediT roles; exact manuscript version; data and synthetic-data statements; source and archived software release; environment and workflow; figure provenance; protocol and preregistration; ethics, consent and governance; funding and interests; accessibility; preservation; external reviews; suggested review communities; and known limitations.

## What happens next

Deterministic checks and approved AI-assisted screening may identify remediation needs. An editor confirms triage, assesses scope and ethics, chooses a review route, and remains responsible for reviewer appointment and the final decision. Passing screening is not acceptance.

## Consent and confidentiality

A future form must separately explain AI use, inputs, retention, providers, human review, correction rights and alternatives. Do not submit confidential or restricted material until JORN publishes an approved privacy notice and secure route.

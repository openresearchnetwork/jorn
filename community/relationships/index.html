Home/Relationship register

Community

Name recommendations, recognition, integrations, and partnerships accurately.

This register will disclose every confirmed external relationship, its scope, evidence, data flows, conflicts, decision owner and current status.

Current register: no confirmed entries. Services and organisations named elsewhere are examples for investigation. None is a JORN partner, recognised provider or integration unless listed here with evidence.

Four categories¶

Category Meaning Minimum publication evidence
Recommended service May help authors meet a requirement; no formal relationship Selection criteria, review date, limitations and conflicts
Recognised assessment provider Specified assessment may be accepted for a named dimension Recognition decision, qualifying scope, criteria and review date
Integrated infrastructure provider Documented technical integration or metadata exchange Data-flow, security, responsibility, exit and incident documentation
Formal partner Signed or formally approved relationship Responsibilities, duration, branding, data handling, conflicts and independence safeguards

Register fields¶

Every entry must name the provider, category, purpose, start and review dates, approver, evidence, services or assessments covered, technical and personal-data flows, cost, conflicts, branding rights, editorial boundary, status and change history. No provider can pay for preferential recommendation, recognition, routing or certification.
